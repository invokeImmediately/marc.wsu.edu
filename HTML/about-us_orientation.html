Orientation

Every fall semester during WSU’s Week of Welcome, the MARC-WSU program will have an orientation workshop that serves to inform current and new scholars and their faculty mentors about MARC-WSU events for the new academic year. This meeting is also a place where scholars, faculty, and staff share their suggestions on improving the program. The official ceremony inducting new scholars into the program will be held at the end of the orientation program each year in August.
